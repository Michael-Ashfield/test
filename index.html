Getting here

We are situated in the heart of Southampton city centre.

By car

During open day events there will be no parking on the main University campus apart from blue badge holders. The nearest car parks are located about 10 minutes' walk away, at Grosvenor Square (SO15 2BE) and Bedford Place (SO15 2DS). If you are a blue badge holder please email/call us to reserve a parking space on the main campus.

By train

The nearest train station is Southampton Central, www.nationalrail.co.uk.

You can book a seat on our station shuttle which will bring you to the main campus, please email/call us.

Travel voucher scheme

Guests who live outside the local authority of Southampton City Council can apply to receive an Amazon voucher that covers the cost of their travel (up to the value of \'a350). For more information and to make an application, please email or call.
